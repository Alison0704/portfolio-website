Microwave Amplifier 2.8GHz

Designed and simulated a microwave amplifier operating at 2.8GHz using Cadence AWR. The design process involved selecting appropriate transistor models, biasing networks, and impedance matching to achieve optimal gain and stability. Simulation results demonstrated a gain of approximately 15dB with a bandwidth of 500MHz, making it suitable for applications in wireless communication systems.
